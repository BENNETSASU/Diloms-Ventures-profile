Dilloms Ventures

Dilloms Ventures, established in 2002, pioneered the telecommunications industry in Ghana with innovative solutions. Initially, we specialized in: - Supplying and distributing prepaid scratch cards - Installing and maintaining landlines - Selling mobile phones Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction propelled our growth. We became a trusted name in the telecom sector.

In 2015, we diversified our portfolio by venturing into the oil and gas industry, expanding our services to include: - LPG gas cylinder distribution - Oil and gas products and services This strategic move leveraged our existing infrastructure and expertise, enabling us to provide comprehensive energy solutions to our customers. Our LPG gas cylinder distribution network rapidly expanded, ensuring reliable and safe energy access to households, businesses, and communities. Throughout our journey, we've remained committed to innovation, customer satisfaction, and sustainability.
